Course Scheduler

An AI-powered scheduling assistant built collaboratively with international partners.

Project Overview

About

The Course Scheduler is a conversational AI tool designed to help University of Dayton students plan their upcoming semesters with less guesswork. Instead of digging through course catalogs, prerequisite trees, and degree audits in separate places, students can describe what they've taken and what they're aiming for, and the assistant returns a recommended schedule tailored to their progress.

Built as part of a Collaborative Online International Learning (COIL) project between students at the University of Dayton and international partners, the system is designed to feel approachable while reasoning over real catalog data. Our goal is to take the friction out of academic planning so students can focus on the actual decisions that matter.

Input
Tell the AI your current courses, year, and major.
Reasoning
The agent checks prerequisites and degree requirements.
Output
A suggested schedule with rationale you can iterate on.
